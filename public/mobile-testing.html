Mobile Features

  • Simplified Interface: Only essential controls are shown - play button, speed control, and cancel button
  • Touch Optimization: Larger touch targets, mobile-friendly input, adaptive styling
  • Swipe Navigation:
    • Swipe left to proceed to the next sentence
    • Swipe right to go back to the previous sentence
    • Swipe up to replay the current sentence
    • Swipe down to cycle through playback speeds
  • Mobile-Optimized Feedback: Results screen adjusted for better mobile viewing
  • Keyboard Shortcuts Hidden: No keyboard shortcut panel on mobile devices
